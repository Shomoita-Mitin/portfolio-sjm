The path

One researcher. Five chapters. One meaningful destination.

Follow the girl along the path as observation becomes a question, the question becomes a model, and the model becomes a published contribution.

1

Alohomora Gait

The human story behind the data

The journey begins in the lab, where neurological data is no longer only a collection of numbers. Each signal represents a person experiencing movement, uncertainty, and moments when the body suddenly refuses to move.

  • Observe healthy participants and people living with Parkinson’s disease.
  • Understand the emotional and physical impact of Freezing of Gait.
  • Ask whether data can distinguish healthy, Parkinson’s, and freezing-related patterns.
2

Accio Attention

Finding movement inside stillness

A difficult question changes the direction of the research: can gait impairment be recognized while a person is resting, even when no visible movement is taking place?

  • Review previous neurological and machine-learning research.
  • Test traditional machine-learning and deep-learning approaches.
  • Shift toward a Transformer architecture with multiple self-attention layers.

3

Wingardium Leviosa

Raising the work beyond a baseline

The research is challenged to answer a harder question: what does this work contribute that is genuinely new? Instead of stopping at one model, the exploration expands across channels, configurations, and data modalities.

  • Reconsider assumptions and expected outcomes.
  • Vary the number of EEG channels and input modalities.
  • Develop and evaluate twelve separate model configurations.

4

Expecto Patronum

Strength through collaboration

Research becomes stronger when it is examined by others. Mentors, co-authors, scholars, and reviewers reveal blind spots that are difficult to see from inside the work.

  • Accept thoughtful criticism as part of rigorous research.
  • Refine the clarity, methodology, and impact of the work.
  • Recognize collaboration as protection against uncertainty and unseen weaknesses.
5

Lumos Maxima

Light after uncertainty

After two and a half years of uncertainty, experimentation, mentorship, and persistence, the research reaches publication. The outcome represents more than a paper; it marks resilience, growth, and the people who helped illuminate the path.

  • Continue through moments of isolation and doubt.
  • Allow mentorship and collaboration to provide direction.
  • Celebrate publication as both a scientific and personal milestone.

The destination was never reached alone.

It was shaped by the people behind the data, the mentors who redirected the work, the collaborators who strengthened it, and the persistence required to keep walking when the path was unclear.

After all this time: Always.
