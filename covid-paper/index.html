The research problem

Lower severity does not mean the threat is gone

Although the severity of COVID-19 has decreased in many countries, pandemics remain an ongoing global concern. Reliable methods are needed to determine severity and compare stages over time.

🌍

Continuing global threat

COVID-19 remains a challenge even after major waves have declined in many regions.

Severity parameters

Four dimensions of pandemic impact

The study evaluates pandemic severity using variables that represent not only disease outcomes, but also the wider social effects of the pandemic.

1

Economy

Measures economic disruption and pandemic-related financial impact.

2

Education

Examines interruption to education, learning access, and institutional activity.

3

Physical health

Represents disease conditions and physical health consequences.

4

Mental health

Includes psychological and emotional effects associated with the pandemic.
