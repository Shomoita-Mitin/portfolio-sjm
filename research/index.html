The clinical challenge

When walking suddenly stops

Parkinson’s disease can affect both movement and cognition. Freezing of gait causes temporary episodes in which a person intends to move, but their feet appear unable to continue.

🚶

Gait dysfunction

Freezing episodes can interrupt walking, increase fall risk, and reduce independence.

👁

Subjective assessment

Traditional evaluation may depend on observation, patient reports, or clinician interpretation.

⚙

Specialized equipment

Existing gait-analysis methods may require dedicated facilities, sensors, or complex testing.

Model architecture

Bi-cephalic Self-Attention Model

BiSAM uses two coordinated processing heads to learn meaningful relationships across signal and descriptive features. Self-attention helps the model emphasize the information most relevant to classification.

  • Learns from multiple sources rather than relying on a single feature type.
  • Uses attention to prioritize informative relationships.
  • Supports efficient classification with reduced EEG channel sets.
